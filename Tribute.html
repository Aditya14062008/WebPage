A Tribute Page

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born and raised in Rameswaram,Tamil Nadu and studied Physics and Aerospace Engineering. He was born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram.He has written many books like Wings of Fire, Ignited Minds, Naa Jeevana, Turning points, Adamya Sahas, You are born to blossom etc. He did his enegeering course and passed out of Madras Institute of Technology. He had recieved awards like The Bharat Ratna, Padma Vibhushan, Padma Bhushan etc. He was also known as the Missile Man of India for his work on the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology. He also played a pivotal organisational, technical, and political role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998, the first since the original nuclear test by India in 1974. He had also written the accomplishments India could have in 2020 in his book India's Vision 2020. Unfortunatly the book was incomplete as he had passed away before he could finish it.One of his famous quote was-

“Don't take rest after your first victory because if you fail in second, more lips are waiting to say that your first victory was just luck.”

He passed away on 27th July 2015 in Shillong while he was giving a speech in a school.He was working until his last breath of his life.
